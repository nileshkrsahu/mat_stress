Mental Arithmetic Task

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. You will be shown arithmetic problems to solve.
  2. There are four levels with increasing difficulties.
  3. You have a maximum of 4 seconds, 4 seconds, 5 seconds, and 5 seconds for levels 1, 2, 3, and 4, respectively.
  4. You have to choose one option out of four.
  5. If you make a mistake, the screen will turn red to remind you to be careful.
  6. Try to maximize your score to be at the top of the scoreboard.
  7. For each correct answer, you will get +1 point, and -1 point for a wrong answer.
